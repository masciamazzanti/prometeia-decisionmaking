Self-Check

Thirty everyday work situations. For each one, choose what you would actually do — not what you think is expected. There are no right or wrong answers.

30 situations ~8 minutes Private — nothing leaves this screen

At the end you'll see how your instincts spread across four ways of responding when something lands in your lap. Bring the result to the workshop — that's the whole point.

Accountability isn't a personality — it's the call you make in the grey zone between what's clearly yours and what's clearly not. This isn't a score; it's a mirror. These four are broad postures, not precise labels — notice which one you fall back on when you don't take the lead. That gap is where the workshop starts.
